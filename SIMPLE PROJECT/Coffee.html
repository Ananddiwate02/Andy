What is Coffee?

Coffee is a brewed drink made from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. It's known for its rich flavor and stimulating effects due to caffeine.

Types of Coffee

  • Espresso
  • Americano
  • Latte
  • Cappuccino
  • Mocha

Health Benefits of Coffee

Moderate coffee consumption has been linked to various health benefits, including:

  1. Improved energy levels
  2. Enhanced mental performance
  3. Antioxidant properties
  4. Reduced risk of certain diseases
